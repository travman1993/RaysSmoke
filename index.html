Veteran Owned

Owner & Pitmaster

Meet Ray

Ray spent years serving his country before he ever picked up a pair of tongs. When he came home, he brought the same discipline, patience, and attention to detail to the smoker that he did to everything else in his life.

What started as backyard cookouts for friends and family turned into something bigger — a trailer parked on West Ave, hardwood smoke rolling from sunup, and a line of neighbors who come back every single weekend.

For Ray, barbecue isn't fast food. It's a craft built on low heat, long hours, and doing right by the people who show up hungry.
